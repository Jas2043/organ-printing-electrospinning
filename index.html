Chemicals & Solutions needed:

  • Soy protein isolate
  • Alginate-protein isolate
  • Gelatin isolate
  • Polyvinyl alcohol (PVA)
  • Formic acid
  • HFIP (Hexafluoroisopropanol)
  • Distilled water

The Electrospinning Setup and testing set up:

  • Syringe pump
  • High-voltage power supply of a maximum of 20V
  • Needle (22G-30G)
  • Aluminum foil or rotating drum collector
  • Characterization Equipment
  • Tensile testing machine
  • Thermal conductivity testing setup (thermocouples, alcohol burner)

Preparation of Electrospinning Solutions:

  1. Prepare Soy Protein, Alginate-Protein, Gelatin, and PVA isolates.
  2. Mix 100 milligrams of each protein with 0.5 mL formic acid and 50 mg of hexadecyltrimethyl ammoniumbromide.
  3. Stir the four new solutions with an ultrasonic mixer or by hand for about 3 minutes until they are fully incorporated.

Electrospinning Process:

  1. Rinse the syringe and put each solution into a syringe attached to a syringe pump.
  2. Set up the electrospinning parameters with Voltage: 10–30 kV, Flow rate: 0.5mL/h, Needle-collector distance: 10–20 cm
  3. Collect fibers on an aluminum foil or rotating drum collector with a rotate speed 2400 RPM...
